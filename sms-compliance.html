SMS Opt-In Workflow

This page demonstrates the 10DLC compliant opt-in process for the CrateLuv pet safety application.

1. Non-Mandatory Opt-In Demonstration

In accordance with carrier requirements, CrateLuv utilizes a voluntary opt-in mechanism. Users are not forced to receive SMS messages to use the platform. The SMS toggle is unchecked by default.

2. Compliance Language

The following disclosure is presented at every point of phone number collection:

"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from CrateLuv. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. View our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service."

3. Data Protection

CrateLuv maintains a strict policy regarding originator data. No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.
