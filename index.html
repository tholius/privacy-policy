CrateLuv

Automated safety and wellness monitoring for home and pet owners.

How it Works

CrateLuv provides automated check-in alerts. Users opt-in to receive SMS notifications regarding device status and safety protocols. In the event of a missed check-in, the system initiates a rescue cascade to your designated emergency contacts.

Our app is currently in private beta. For inquiries, please contact support@crateluv.com.
